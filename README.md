# Monopoly Go Manual Sticker Trade Calculator

A lightweight, fully manual helper tool designed for Monopoly Go players who manage **4 alternate accounts**. This app eliminates the mental math by instantly calculating how to transfer missing cards between your accounts based on your manual inputs.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **100% Manual Input:** Full control over selecting your active account, collection pages, and specific missing card numbers.
*   **Instant Trade Calculation:** Based on your inputs, the app immediately shows the exact trade path (e.g., *Account X ➔ sends to ➔ Account Y*).
*   **No Automation or Tracking:** Safe and independent tool with no connections to the official game or external data tracking.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. **Select Account:** Manually pick your current Account Number.
2. **Choose the Card:** Select the specific Collection Page and the Card Number you are missing.
3. **Get Results:** The app will instantly display the optimal trade route to complete your set.
