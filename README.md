# Monopoly

A simple and lightweight helper tool for Monopoly Go players who manage 4 alternate accounts. This is a fully manual calculator that helps you quickly determine how to transfer missing cards between your accounts without doing the mental math yourself.
​Key Features:
​Fully Manual Input: Complete control over selecting account numbers, collection pages, and specific missing card numbers.
​Instant Trade Calculation: Based on your manual selection, the app instantly calculates the exact route (which account needs to send a card to which other account).
​Lightweight & Independent: No automation, no background tracking, and no connection to the game required. You enter the data, it gives you the solution.
​How to Use:
​Manually select your current Account Number.
​Choose the specific Collection Page and the Card Number you are currently missing.
​The app will instantly calculate and show the trade path: [Account X] ➔ sends to ➔ [Account Y].
